Guacamole

Decription

This is the best guacamole recipe that I have come across. A good guacamole is simple but is bursting with flavor. The key is to use the ingredients in a way that highlight instead over power the avocado. This recipe does just that! One taste of this recipe and it will leave you hooked and craving for more! Enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados- pitted, peeled, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon of sale
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 3 tablespoons cilantro
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic
  • 1 pinch of ground pepper

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt
  2. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, garlic
  3. stir in pepper
  4. Refrigerate for 1 hour for best flavor, serve immediately
